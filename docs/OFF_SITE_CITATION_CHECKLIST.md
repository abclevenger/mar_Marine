## Review generation (manual)

- Do not post fake reviews. Do not offer incentives that violate platform policies.
- **Review prompts** (for real customers, optional):
  - “What service did you use? (e.g., wet slip, dry storage, haul-out, repair)”
  - “How long have you been a customer?”
  - “What did you like most?”
- Encourage specific mentions (e.g., “haul-out,” “dry storage,” “Tarpon Springs”) so reviews support local and service SEO.
- Respond professionally to all reviews where the platform allows.
